Bouncer Slide

Jump, bounce, and slide into splashing fun on our exciting inflatable attraction

About This Attraction

The Bouncer Slide combines the excitement of a bouncy castle with the thrill of a water slide! This colorful inflatable attraction features multiple bouncing sections, climbing walls, and a refreshing water slide finish. It's the perfect spot for kids to burn energy while staying cool in the water.

Features

  • ✓ Large inflatable bouncing area
  • ✓ Climbing wall with soft grips
  • ✓ Curved water slide exit
  • ✓ Continuous water spray for cooling
  • ✓ Soft landing splash zone
  • ✓ Great for ages 5-12

Safety Requirements

Age Range: 5-12 years old (recommended)
Weight Limit: Maximum 150 lbs per person
Capacity: Maximum 8 children at a time
Rules: No shoes, glasses, or sharp objects allowed
